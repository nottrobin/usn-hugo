### ghostscript vulnerability

A security issue affects these releases of Ubuntu and its derivatives:

* Ubuntu 10.04 LTS
* Ubuntu 8.04 LTS

### Summary

Ghostscript could be made to crash or run programs as your login if it opened a specially crafted file.

* ghostscript
